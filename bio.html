Bio

I was born in Brasov, Romania. I lived there for 18 years there. I finished one of the most prestigious high school from my country, however when the time came, I felt that there is so much more outside of Brasov, outside of Romania. That was the time when I have applied for an AP degree in Computer Science at the Copenhagen School of Design and Technology./

One year later, I realised that there is still so much more to be explored, to be uncovered, to be understood and to be learned, so I am on my way to spend a semester in Canada, studying yet again Computer Science.

As you might have noticed by now, my interests revolve around computers and the digital world. I am an avid reader and I generally think that it is essential for me, and humans in general, to learn something new everyday. I am keen on proving myself to me, and to everybody else. That is why I would rather not do something if I know that I will not be involved in it 100%.

My hobbies are reading, writing and studying new programming languages. I also enjoy cooking and luckily I have been working for the last year and a half as a cook in an Italian restaurant. I want to make the most out of my time and I am open minded when it comes to trying new things...
